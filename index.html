Who am I?

If you're here, this is probably the first question you'll be asking.
I'm Miguel, I'm 26 and I'm from Portugal. I've moved to Manchester to pursue a career as a Nuclear Medicine Technologist.

How I got into code?

Easy. I was bored. Yes, that's right. There is no exciting story about how I found myself on the verge of a breaking down and in need to change my life completely, and how code save it. Basically, while at work, I did my normal duties and then found myself with a few empty hours before the end of my shift, which bored me to dead. I started having a look into options of what to learn next.

I've always felt that my job took away my creative side, which was were I could excel. And, to be honest, I was very eager to bring it back.

Since then, I've completed the Build Responsive Real World Websites with HTML5 and CSS3 course by Jonas Schmedtmann, which entirely reinforced my idea of applying to the Manchester Codes Bootcamp.
